Welcome to the summer soltice festival 2024!

    We are honored to bring you this years summer soltice festival! so come down and bring your loved ones with for some summer fun!

    The summer soltice is about intending to boost the sun's strength for the remainder of the crop season and ensure a healthy harvest. it is the longest day of our whole year. it is a symbol to bring loved ones along to celebrate the strength the sun gives us and to heal ourselves.
